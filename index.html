Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal advice?

No. DemandAI provides document generation services, not legal advice. Attorney review does not constitute legal advice or establish an attorney-client relationship.

How long will it take to receive my letter?

Basic and Professional letters are delivered immediately. Attorney-Reviewed letters are typically delivered within 1-2 business days.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Though you won't see the letter before paying, if you're not satisfied with the quality of your letter for any reason, contact us within 24 hours at support@demandai.com for a full refund.

Will an attorney represent me in court?

No. Our service is limited to generating demand letters. It does not include representation in legal proceedings.

How is my payment information protected?

All payments are processed securely through Stripe, one of the world's most trusted payment processors. Your credit card details are never stored on our servers.

Can I download my letter as a Word document?

Yes! For all service tiers, you can download your letter as a Word document immediately after purchase, allowing you to make any minor edits you need.

Do you use AI to generate letters?

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to generate custom demand letters tailored to your specific situation. The AI creates professional, legally-formatted letters based on the information you provide.
